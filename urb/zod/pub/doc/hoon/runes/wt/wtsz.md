wutsaz, %wtsz
======================

[`++tiki`]() version of [`%wtsg`]()

`wutsaz` is a synthetic rune that produces `q` if `p` is `~`, `r`
otherwise.

Produces
--------

Twig: `[%wtsz p=tiki q=twig r=twig]`

Sample
------

`p` is a [`++tiki`](). `q` and `r` are [twig]()s.

Tall form
---------

    ?~  p
      q
    r

Wide form
---------

    ?~(p q r)

Irregular form
--------------

None

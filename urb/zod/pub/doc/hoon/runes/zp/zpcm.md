zapcom, `!,`, %zpcm
============================

Insert as constant

`!,` is a natural rune that inserts twig `q` as a constant, typed with
the type of twig `p`.

Produces
--------

Twig: `[%zpcm p=twig q=twig]`

Sample
------

`p` and `q` are [twig]()s.

Tall form
---------

    !,  p
        q

Wide form
---------

    !,(p q)

Irregular form
--------------

None
